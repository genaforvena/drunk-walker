WHAT IS THIS?
Drunk Walker is a chaotic "blind" navigator for Google Street View. It doesn't know where it's going, but it's determined to get there.

🌀 Blind Chaos: It clicks where it thinks "forward" is, creating a mesmerizing automated journey.
🖱️ Steer with Mouse: Point your cursor anywhere to "guide" the walker in that direction.
⏸ Smart Pausing: It stops clicking automatically when you drag the mouse to look around.
🎚️ Adjustable Pace: Use the on-screen slider to change the walking speed.
HOW TO RUN (Developer Console):
1. Open Google Maps Street View.
2. Press F12 (or Right-Click > Inspect) and go to the Console tab.
3. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) and press Enter.
4. Type allow pasting if your browser asks, then paste again.
5. Click anywhere on the map to guide the walker!
Works on any browser. No extension required.
