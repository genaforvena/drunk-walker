🤪 DRUNK WALKER

THE "NO-INSTALL" STREET VIEW CHAOS ENGINE

Drunk Walker transforms Google Street View into a mesmerizing, automated travelogue. It works by blindly clicking around the center of your screen to move you forward, creating an unpredictable journey through the world's streets.
✓ CODE COPIED TO CLIPBOARD!
CORE FEATURES:
🌀 Center-Path Exploration: Automatically clicks or presses keys to navigate forward.
📐 Custom Selection: Draw a polygon to restrict the walker to specific screen regions.
🎚️ Adjustable Pace: Control the walking speed with an on-screen slider.
⏸ Smart Observation: Pauses automatically when you click/drag to look around.
🌀 Experimental Mode: Detects when you are stuck and triggers exponential chaos or panic turns.
⌨️ Keyboard Mode: Toggle to simulate Arrow Up navigation for smoother movement.
🛠️ Zero Install: Runs entirely in your browser console.
HOW TO RUN (Developer Console):
1. Open Google Maps Street View.
2. Press F12 (or Right-Click > Inspect) and go to the Console tab.
3. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) and press Enter.
4. Type allow pasting if your browser asks, then paste again.
5. Click anywhere on the map to guide the walker!
Works on any browser. No extension required.
